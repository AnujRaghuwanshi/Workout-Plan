My Gym Workout Routine

Monday - Biceps & Forearm

Exercises

  • Barbell Curl - 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Hammer Curl - 3 sets of 12 reps
  • Concentration Curl - 3 sets of 15 reps
  • EZ-bar Curl - 2 sets of 10 reps

Tuesday - Triceps & Forearm

Exercises

  • Tricep Dips - 4 sets of 10-12 reps
  • Dumbbell French Press - 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Tricep Dumbbell kickback - 4 sets of 10-15 reps

Wednesday - Shoulders

Exercises

  • Overhead Press - 3 sets of 8 reps
  • Lateral Raises - 3 sets of 15 reps
  • Front Raises - 4 sets of 10 reps

Thursday - Back

Exercises

  • Wide Grip lat pulldown - 4 sets of 6 reps
  • Pull-Ups - 4 sets to failure
  • Barbell Rows - 4 sets of 8 reps

Friday - Chest

Exercises

  • Bench Press - 4 sets of 6-8 reps
  • Push-Ups - 3 sets to failure
  • Chest Fly - 4 sets of 12 reps

Saturday - Legs

Exercises

  • Squats - 4 sets of 10 reps
  • Lunges - 3 sets of 12 reps per leg
  • Leg Press - 4 sets of 8 reps
  • Side-Lunge - 3 sets of 10 reps per leg

Sunday - Rest

Today is your rest day! Focus on recovery: stretching, foam rolling, and staying hydrated.
